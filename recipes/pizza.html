Hawaiian Pizza

Special Hawaiian Wood-fired Pizza with ham, sauce, cheese and pineapple

Essential ingredients

  • Pizza dough: The base of the pizza.
  • Pizza sauce: Traditionally a tomato-based sauce.
  • Mozzarella cheese: Provides the classic melted, cheesy layer.
  • Ham: The traditional savory meat, often Canadian bacon.
  • Pineapple: The key sweet and tangy fruit, either fresh or canned chunks.

Common additions

  • Bacon: Adds a smoky, salty flavor and crispy texture.
  • Red onion: Offers a mild, pungent bite.
  • Oregano: A common herb for seasoning pizza.
  • Other seasonings: Salt, pepper, or other herbs can be added to taste.
